The recent acquisition of **Verint Systems** by **Thoma Bravo** for $2 billion underscores the strong demand for AI-driven customer engagement platforms, as companies seek to enhance operational efficiency.

- **Telecommunications:** Telecom companies are investing heavily in 5G infrastructure, which is expected to drive new revenue streams from IoT and enhanced mobile services. The focus on low-latency technologies is pivotal for improving user experiences.

- **AI:** The AI subsector is at the forefront of technological advancements, with applications spanning various industries. Companies are rapidly adopting AI solutions to maintain competitive advantages, particularly in customer service automation.
