This growth is driven by the need for grid stability and increased renewable energy integration.

- **M&A Opportunities:** Companies may pursue acquisitions of energy storage startups to enhance their capabilities. For example, Tesla's acquisition of energy storage technology firms could further solidify its market leadership.
