- **M&A Opportunities:** Acquisitions of precision medicine startups can enhance diagnostic capabilities. Roche's acquisition of Foundation Medicine highlights this trend.

- **Competitive Landscape:** Key players include Novartis (NVS) and Biogen (BIIB), both heavily investing in gene therapy innovations. The market is competitive, pushing firms to acquire unique gene editing capabilities.

- **M&A Opportunities:** Companies may look to acquire startups in CRISPR technology or viral vector development. Novartis's acquisition of AveXis for $8.7 billion exemplifies strategic moves in this space.

#### Telemedicine

@@@ Key Companies:
