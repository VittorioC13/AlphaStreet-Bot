- **M&A Opportunities:** Companies may pursue acquisitions of precision medicine firms to bolster their R&D and diagnostic capabilities. Telix's strategic positioning suggests potential partnerships or acquisitions to expand its product offerings.

- **M&A Opportunities:** Firms may look to acquire gene therapy startups specializing in CRISPR technology or viral vector development to enhance their therapeutic pipelines.

#### Telemedicine
