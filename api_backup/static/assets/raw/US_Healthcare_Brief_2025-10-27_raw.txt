- **M&A Opportunities:** Companies may pursue acquisitions of precision medicine firms to enhance their diagnostic capabilities. Roche's acquisition of Foundation Medicine illustrates the trend towards integrating precision medicine into traditional healthcare.

- **M&A Opportunities:** Companies may consider acquiring firms developing alternative menopause therapies to broaden their product portfolios. Bayer's strategic positioning with Lynkuet may lead to further investments in this area.
