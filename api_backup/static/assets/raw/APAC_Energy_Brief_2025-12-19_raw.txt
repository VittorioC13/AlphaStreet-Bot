For instance, Brent crude fell to $59.50 per barrel, reflecting a broader trend of decreasing demand and economic uncertainty. Companies like BP are feeling the impact, with shares dropping 1.4% alongside weaker oil prices.

- **M&A Opportunities:** Energy companies may pursue acquisitions of energy storage startups to enhance grid capabilities. Tesla's acquisition of Maxwell Technologies indicates a trend towards integrating storage solutions into traditional energy infrastructure.

- **M&A Opportunities:** Companies may consider acquiring startups specializing in niche grid applications, such as demand response or advanced metering infrastructure. Schneider Electric's acquisition of Aveva Group for $5.7 billion in 2022 exemplifies this trend.
