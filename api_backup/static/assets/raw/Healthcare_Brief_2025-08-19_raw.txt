- **Digital Health:** The digital health subsector is witnessing explosive growth, driven by the adoption of AI solutions across various applications. Companies are rapidly developing technologies that enhance diagnostics and patient management, positioning themselves for future success.

@@@ Drivers:
- **Technological Advancements:** Continuous innovation in AI, genomics, and digital health is propelling growth across the healthcare landscape. The integration of AI in drug discovery and patient care is becoming increasingly prevalent, as evidenced by KKR's strategic acquisition.
