- **Trend Explanation:** Urban Air Mobility refers to the development of air transportation systems that facilitate short-distance travel within urban environments. @@@ Key Companies:
  - **Doroni Aerospace:** Doroni is pioneering personal eVTOL aircraft with its H1-X model, designed for everyday users. The aircraft is FAA-certified and aims to revolutionize personal flight with intuitive controls and safety features. Doroni has received over 600 pre-orders, indicating strong market interest.

- **Trend Explanation:** The aerospace manufacturing sector is increasingly adopting advanced technologies such as AI, robotics, and additive manufacturing to enhance production efficiency and product quality. This trend is critical as the global aerospace market is expected to grow significantly, driven by increasing demand for air travel.

@@@ Key Companies:

The facility will utilize cutting-edge technologies and is expected to employ over 2,200 people by 2026, positioning Collins for growth in the aerospace sector.

#### High-Reliability Engineering Solutions
