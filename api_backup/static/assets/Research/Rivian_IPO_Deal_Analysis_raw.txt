- **Date Announced:** November 10, 2021 (IPO debut)
- **Strategic Rationale:** This IPO enables Rivian to access public capital markets to fund its electric vehicle production and expansion plans. The transaction provides Rivian with significant capital to scale manufacturing, develop new models, and compete in the growing electric vehicle market. Additionally, the deal enables Rivian to leverage its Amazon partnership and technology expertise to enhance performance and expand market reach. The IPO also provides significant opportunities through public market access and strategic partnerships in the electric vehicle ecosystem.

The transaction also faces regulatory challenges and potential operational disruptions during the growth phase.

- **Profitability Ratios:** Rivian's EBITDA margin of approximately -15% and net income margin of -25% reflect the early-stage nature of the electric vehicle business and significant investment in growth

- **Asset Operating Efficiency:** Rivian's working capital management shows inventory turnover of 3x and receivables turnover of 4x, reflecting the manufacturing-based nature of electric vehicle operations
- **Valuation Context:** The 45x EV/EBITDA multiple represents a significant premium to traditional automotive trading range but is justified by the growth potential of the electric vehicle market and Rivian's strategic positioning
